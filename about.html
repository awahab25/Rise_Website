The Royal Promise

Founded in the heart of Pakistan's rice belts, Royal Rice Mills was established with a singular vision: to bring the authentic taste and aroma of Pakistani Basmati to the global table.

Over two decades, we have invested in the most advanced milling technology and built a state-of-the-art facility. Today, we are one of the most reliable exporters known for uncompromising quality and timely deliveries across Europe, the Middle East, and Africa.

  • ISO 22000 & HACCP Certified Facility
  • Advanced color sorting & multi-stage milling
  • Moisture-controlled climate storage
  • Private label & custom packaging solutions
  • FOB & CIF export terms available

The Beginning

Going International
