Custom Rice Milling

State-of-the-art milling solutions with adjustable whitening and polishing degrees to meet specific market preferences — from highly polished premium to lightly milled health grades.

Reliable bulk supply chain for food manufacturers, hotel chains, hospitals, and government institutions worldwide. Recurring supply contracts with price-lock options available.
