Uncompromising Quality Control

Quality isn't just a department at Royal Rice Mills — it's our core philosophy. We implement rigorous testing at every stage of production.

Laboratory Testing

In-house lab analysis of grain length, moisture content, broken percentage, purity, aroma, and color for every single batch before dispatch.

Hygienic Handling

Our facility follows strict HACCP protocols ensuring zero contamination from paddy intake right through to final bagging and sealing.

Batch Traceability

Every shipment carries a unique batch code traceable back to its specific processing date, milling run, source farm region, and paddy lot.

International Standards

Our processing meets the food safety requirements of the EU, USA (FDA), GCC, and African markets, accepted by leading global importers.
